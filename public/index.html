About us

Voodoo Krewe is a team with both woodsball and speedball players based at Paintball Command in Mandeville, Louisiana.

Voodoo Krewe was created when PBC Army and SAS Louisiana merged together, creating a team that is the greatest in the area. Throughout the early stages of the team, new members rolled in little by little! The resulting team is proudly sponsored by Paintball Command.
