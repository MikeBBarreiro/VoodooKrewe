About us

We have guys that do Woodsball, we have guys that do speed ball, and we also have guys that do both!

The Origins of VoodooKrewe actually comes from 2 different teams, forming into one strong team consisting the greatest in the area. Once PBC Army and SAS Louisiana combinded a paintball confederation of sorts. VK went through many different names.

Throughout the time of re-naming the team and finding a solid ground of how to invest on the teams theme and what we would stand for, members started rolling in little by little! The end result. VOODOOKREWE, proud sponser by PBC (Paintball Command) and new upcoming opporunities.
