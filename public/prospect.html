VK PROSPECT PROCEDURE

Prospect must be presented to Elders by an existing VK member.

Hello, and welcome to the Prospect Procedure! Prospect will have one calendar year to obtain 10 VC (Voodoo Credits) or 15 VC in a two-year time in order to be voted upon.


Event point values:

2-day pbc scenario: 3 VC

1-day pbc scenario: 2 VC

"Rec Day"***: 1 VC

*** Rec Days are and Saturday or Sunday at PBC where the prospect actively plays beside 2 present elders for a minimun of 4 hours of the day. In the event of more than one rec day being used for VC , the prospect must have played beside 4 elders throughout their trial period.
Once prospect has achieved their goal in the timeframe, the entirety of VK will vote. If the vote totaled above 50%, yes.., the elders vote. Prospect must have a vote of 3:2 in order to be inducted. In the event of a 2:3 vote, the prospect may achieve 5 additional VC in the 6 months following the vote. Once 5 additional VC are earned, a revote by VK and the elders will take place. Thank you- and long live the elders!
