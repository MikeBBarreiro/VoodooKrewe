Hello, and welcome to the Prospect Procedure! When presented by a current Voodoo Krewe Member to an Officer, all it takes is 2 weekends of rec day gamplay or one scenario under the eye of Voodoo Krewe members. Please note.. These prospect procudures might change. So please check back frequently or just get in contact for more information.


*** Rec Days are on Saturdays or Sundays at Paintball Command where the prospect actively plays beside any of the Voodoo Krewe Members for a minimun of 4 hours of the day. The prospect must have played beside a minumum of 2 officers throughout their trial period. It is important that an Officer reviews the prospects abilities during gameplay and characteristics at the staging area.
Once prospect has achieved their goal in the timeframe, Officers will meet at round table and gether information on the prospects Skill , Dedication , and most importantly, Character . During this process Officers get feedback from the current Voodoo Krewe members. The Officer will then make a final vote. Thank you you for your interest in the Voodoo Krewe Family!
